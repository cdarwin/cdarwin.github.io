I am currently employed as a Practice Leader for the consulting firm, Taos. Through Taos, I have worked on interesting projects for Intuit, Skybox, Jobvite, and Apple. As a Practice Leader, I am responsible for a number of internal training programs and mentorships. I am also responsible for a partnership with YearUp where we provide young adults with entry level training in Linux, Apache, MySQL, and Python.

I have over 10 years of experience in various areas of systems engineering. Some of my strengths include:

  • Infrastructure architecture and design including bare metal, virtualization, public and private cloud environments
  • Configuration Management tools such as puppet, chef, and ansible
  • Build automation, continuous integration, and continuous delivery
  • Programming languages such as C, Java, Go, Bourne Shell, Python, and Ruby
